## AI for Good: Values in Technology

**Project: Build with a human value in mind.** Today you will use the Values at Play framework to plan a small project that supports something people care about.

### The Task
Choose one human value to center in your project, such as privacy, accessibility, trust, autonomy, belonging, or safety.

1. Identify the user or community the project is for.
2. Write down the value you are trying to support and why it matters.
3. Sketch or build a small prototype that makes that value visible in the interface or behavior.
4. Note at least one way the project could fail or create unintended harm.

This can be a low-fidelity prototype, a planning page, or an early version of a site or app. The goal is to make your reasoning explicit.

### Submission: The Vibe Report
After the activity, document your process in `vibe-report.md`.

Your report should include:
1. The value you chose and why.
2. Who benefits from the design and who might be affected by it.
3. How the Values at Play framework changed your plan.
4. What tradeoffs you noticed while building or planning.
5. A link to `log_deliverable/history.md` or other AI usage trace.
6. The course authenticity statement.

### Deliverables
- `code_deliverable/` with the prototype or planning artifact
- `log_deliverable/` with notes, transcripts, or screenshots
- `vibe-report.md` with your reflection and trace
