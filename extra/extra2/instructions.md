## Extra Credit 2: Ethical Intervention

### Folder Structure
Use this structure for your deliverables:

```text
extra/extra2/
├── code_deliverable/
├── log_deliverable/
└── vibe-report.md
```

---

## The Task

Make one concrete ethical improvement to an existing project or webpage.

Choose one of the following directions, or propose a similar one:

1. Improve accessibility.
2. Add or clarify attribution.
3. Reduce a privacy risk.
4. Make a hidden assumption or bias visible.

Your change should be small enough to understand, but clear enough to show why it matters.

## Submission: The Vibe Report

Document your work in `vibe-report.md`.

Your report should include:
1. What you changed.
2. Why the change matters ethically.
3. What tradeoff you made, if any.
4. What users benefit from the change.
5. A link to your `log_deliverable/history.md` or other trace.
6. The course authenticity statement.

### Deliverables
- `code_deliverable/` with the revised site or component
- `log_deliverable/` with notes, screenshots, or transcripts
- `vibe-report.md` with your analysis and reflection

---

*Small design choices can have large ethical consequences.*
