How the numbers work · click to expand

AI side (per unit)

  • Chat query: 0.3 Wh, ~40 ml water, ~0.15 g CO₂e
    — Epoch AI 2025
  • Vibe coding hour: ~50 Wh, ~2.5 L water, ~20 g CO₂e
    — extrapolated from Luccioni et al., FAccT '24 (code generation ~5–10× text classification)
  • Image generation: ~1.5 Wh, ~50 ml water, ~0.6 g CO₂e
    — Luccioni 2024 (image gen ~5–20× text)
  • Water per query: ~10–50 ml midpoint
    — Li et al., CACM 2025 ("Making AI Less Thirsty")

Offset side (per swap)

  • Beef → veggie meal: ~9 kg CO₂e
    — Poore & Nemecek, Science 2018
  • Chicken → tofu meal: ~1.4 kg CO₂e
    — Poore & Nemecek 2018 via Our World in Data
  • Car mile → bike mile: ~380 g CO₂e
    — EPA, typical passenger vehicle (~404 g/mi) minus bike LCA ~21 g/km
  • Skip a NYC–Boston flight: ~75 kg CO₂e per passenger
    — ICAO Carbon Calculator (~0.25 kg/passenger-km × 300 km)
  • 5-min shorter shower: ~500 g CO₂e
    — ~9 L/min × 5 min, 30°C heating ≈ 1.4 kWh × 400 g/kWh (US grid)
  • Cold-wash a load: ~1.5 kg CO₂e
    — hot vs. cold ΔE ~4 kWh × 400 g/kWh
  • Plant a tree (1 yr): ~25 kg CO₂e
    — USDA Forest Service mature-tree annual sequestration
  • Swap incandescent → LED (1 yr): ~22 kg CO₂e
    — 60 W → 9 W × 3 hr/day × 365 d × 400 g/kWh

All numbers are public-domain order-of-magnitude estimates and round generously. Per-query AI footprint varies 2–4× by region (water especially) and by model size. Offsets vary 2–5× by route, vehicle, diet substitution, climate, and heating fuel. Do not present these as exact. If you adapt this calculator, replace the constants with values defensible for your context and cite them.
