## Prompt Engineering Lab

**Project: Make your prompt do the work.** Today you will improve your Week 1 site by changing the way you ask the AI for help and comparing the results.

### The Task
Use prompt iteration to improve the website you started on Week 1 Day 1.

1. Start from your existing site or a fresh version of it.
2. Write at least 3 different prompts that try to improve the design, structure, or content.
3. Compare what changed between each prompt and keep notes on what worked.
4. Choose the strongest direction and finish a cleaner version of the site.

Focus on how prompt wording changes the output. Try at least one prompt that is vague, one that is specific, and one that adds a hard constraint.

### Submission: The Vibe Report
After finishing your iteration, document your process in `vibe-report.md`.

Your report should include:
1. A brief showcase of the final site.
2. The prompt sequence you used.
3. What changed from prompt to prompt.
4. What the AI did well, what it got wrong, and how you corrected it.
5. A short reflection on how prompt engineering changed your workflow.
6. A link to your `log_deliverable/history.md` or other trace of the interaction.
7. The course authenticity statement.

### Deliverables
- `code_deliverable/` with the revised site
- `log_deliverable/` with prompt notes, transcripts, or screenshots
- `vibe-report.md` with your reflection and trace
